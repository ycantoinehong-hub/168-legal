## Common questions

### How do I disconnect a calendar or health source?
In the app, go to **Settings → Imports**, tap the source, and tap **Disconnect**.

### How do I delete all my data?
Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. There is no cloud copy.

### How do I turn off notifications?
iOS **Settings → Notifications → 168**, or in the app go to **Settings → Notifications**.

### Does 168 work without an internet connection?
Yes. The app is fully local. Internet is only needed if you connect Google Calendar.
